RetailMax — National Retail Chain

Challenge

Managing maintenance and repairs across 2,000+ retail locations with unreliable third-party contractors. Inconsistent response times, poor first-time fix rates, and zero visibility into technician performance were costing the company millions in lost productivity and tenant escalations.

Solution

Wurqly deployed its managed services platform with a network of vetted, local technicians across all RetailMax markets. Each technician was matched by skill set, proximity, and past performance scores, with real-time SLA tracking visible to RetailMax's operations team.

Technology

TechServ Solutions — IT Infrastructure Company

Challenge

TechServ needed to scale its technician network from 150 to 500+ across 38 states while maintaining strict quality standards. Their manual dispatch process was creating 4-hour average response delays, and technician churn was running at 45% annually, forcing constant re-hiring and re-training.

Solution

Wurqly's smart scheduling engine and mobile platform replaced TechServ's manual workflow. Automated dispatch matched jobs to the nearest qualified technician in seconds. The mobile app gave field teams real-time work orders, documentation tools, and performance dashboards that improved engagement and reduced turnover.

Real Estate

GreenBuild Properties — Commercial Real Estate

Challenge

GreenBuild managed 42 commercial properties with a purely reactive maintenance approach. HVAC failures, plumbing emergencies, and electrical faults were generating constant tenant complaints. Emergency service calls accounted for over 60% of their maintenance budget, and tenant satisfaction scores had dropped to 3.1 out of 5.

Solution

Wurqly integrated its predictive maintenance module with GreenBuild's building management systems. IoT sensors monitored critical equipment around the clock, flagging anomalies before they became failures. Automated work orders routed to qualified technicians, and the cost analytics dashboard gave property managers clear ROI visibility.
