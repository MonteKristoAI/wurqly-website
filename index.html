Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Wurqly's platform, pricing, and how we work with field service teams.

Wurqly is the only field service platform built on a 0% take rate model. While competitors take 15-30% from every technician job, we charge businesses a flat monthly subscription. This means technicians keep 100% of what they earn, which drives higher retention, better service quality, and stronger customer satisfaction. We combine this fair compensation model with AI-powered scheduling, predictive maintenance, and a mobile-first app built specifically for technicians working in the field.
Instead of taking a percentage from each service job, Wurqly charges businesses a predictable monthly platform fee based on team size and feature tier. Technicians receive their full service rate without any platform deductions. This model aligns incentives across the board: businesses get predictable costs, technicians get fair pay, and customers get motivated service professionals. The result is lower turnover, higher quality, and a more sustainable workforce.
Wurqly serves field service organizations across retail, hospitality, healthcare, manufacturing, commercial real estate, and education. Our platform works for teams of 5 to 5,000+ technicians. Typical customers include facility management companies, HVAC service providers, electrical contractors, plumbing firms, commercial maintenance companies, and multi-location retail chains that need reliable on-site service.
Most teams are fully operational within 2-5 business days. Our onboarding team handles data migration from your existing platform, configures scheduling rules and service territories, and provides hands-on training for dispatchers and technicians. Larger deployments with complex integrations typically take 2-3 weeks. Every customer gets a dedicated success manager to guide the transition.
The Wurqly mobile app was designed for field technicians, not office workers. Most techs are comfortable with the core features within 15-20 minutes. The interface is built for one-thumb operation, works offline in areas with poor connectivity, and syncs automatically when signal returns. We provide in-app walkthroughs, video tutorials, and live training sessions for teams that want deeper onboarding.
Wurqly integrates with the tools field service teams already use: ServiceNow, Salesforce, SAP, Oracle, Zendesk, HubSpot, Stripe, Twilio, QuickBooks, and more. We offer native API connections, webhook support, and a Zapier integration for custom workflows. Our IoT module connects with major sensor platforms for predictive maintenance data. If you need a custom integration, our team builds it at no extra cost during onboarding.
