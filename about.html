About Wurqly

Built on a simple belief: when technicians win, everyone wins.

Our Mission

We built Wurqly because the field service industry had it backwards.

Platforms were squeezing technicians, and businesses were paying the price in quality. We built something different - a model where technicians keep 100% of their earnings, attracting top talent that delivers exceptional results.

The result? Businesses get better technicians, faster response times, and higher customer satisfaction. Technicians get the respect and compensation they deserve. That is the Wurqly difference.

  • 0% take rate - technicians keep every dollar they earn
  • Higher quality talent attracted to a fair platform
  • Better outcomes for businesses and their customers
Our Values

What Drives Everything We Build

Three principles guide every product decision, partnership, and platform update at Wurqly.

People First

Every decision starts with the technician experience. When the people doing the work are supported and fairly compensated, the quality of service follows naturally.

Radical Transparency

No hidden fees, no maze-like pricing tiers, no surprises on your invoice. What you see is what you pay. We publish our pricing because we have nothing to hide.

Relentless Quality

Higher standards attract better technicians. We vet every professional on our platform because your reputation depends on the people we send through your door.
